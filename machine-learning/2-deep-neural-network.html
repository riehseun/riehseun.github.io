Deep neural network

  • \( L \) is the number of layers, \( n^{[l]} \) is the number of hidden units in layer \( l \)

Single training example with 4 hidden layers

  • \( \textbf{z}^{[1]} = \textbf{w}^{[1]}\textbf{x} + \textbf{b}^{[1]} \)
  • \( \textbf{a}^{[1]} = g^{[1]}{(\textbf{z}^{[1]})} \)
  • \( \textbf{z}^{[2]} = \textbf{w}^{[2]}\textbf{a}^{[1]} + \textbf{b}^{[2]} \)
  • \( \textbf{a}^{[2]} = g^{[2]}{(\textbf{z}^{[2]})} \)
  • \( \textbf{z}^{[4]} = \textbf{w}^{[4]}\textbf{a}^{[3]} + \textbf{b}^{[4]} \)
  • \( \textbf{a}^{[4]} = g^{[4]}{(\textbf{z}^{[4]})} = \hat{y} \)

In general, single training example

  • \( \textbf{z}^{[l]} = \textbf{w}^{[l]}\textbf{a}^{[l-1]} + \textbf{b}^{[l]} \)
  • \( \textbf{a}^{[l]} = g^{[l]}{(\textbf{z}^{[l]})} \)

In general, m training examples

  • \( \textbf{Z}^{[l]} = \textbf{W}^{[l]}\textbf{A}^{[l-1]} + \textbf{b}^{[l]} \)
  • \( \textbf{A}^{[l]} = g^{[l]}(\textbf{Z}^{[l]}) \)

Dimensions

  • \( \textbf{X}: (n^{[0]},1) \)
  • \( \textbf{Z}^{[l]}: (n^{[1]},1) \)
