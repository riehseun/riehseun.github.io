Software Engineering

Behavioral

  • Structure
    • Situation - 30 seconds
      • Context
      • Team size
      • Project impact
    • Task - 30 seconds
      • Specific challenges
      • Your roles and responsibilities
      • What success looked like
    • Action - 90 seconds
      • Your contribution
      • Steps that you took
      • Key decisions and rational
      • How you influenced others
    • Result - 30 seconds
      • Quanitify impact
      • Business and technical outcome
      • Lessons learned
  • Conflict Resolution
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Clearly identify who was involved and what they wanted
      • Explain what caused the conflict at its core
      • Describe your specific role (participant, mediator, observer)
      • Demonstrate active listening and empathy
      • Explain how the conflict was resolved
      • Reflect on what you learned
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Focus on strategic approaches to resolution
      • Demonstrate influence and consensus-building
      • Achieve measurable business outcomes
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Address conflicts affecting multiple teams/departments
      • Influence senior stakeholders effectively
      • Identify and address systemic causes
      • Have an effective mental model of your counterparty
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Focus on resolving interpersonal conflicts within your team and improving team dynamics
      • Help coach team members to navigate their own conflicts
      • Reach a conclusion which built trust and respect between parties while also keeping the organization's goals in mind
  • Perseverance
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Clearly define the challenge or obstacle faced
      • Explain why perseverance was required (complexity, failures, resistance)
      • Demonstrate repeated efforts and refusal to give up
      • Show how you adapted your approach based on feedback
      • Highlight determination and resourcefulness
      • Focus on the process, not just the outcome
      • Demonstrate self-awareness about strengths/weaknesses
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Tackle a complex, long-term challenge with significant business consequences
      • Demonstrate data-driven decision making and resourcefulness
      • Show leadership in guiding others through difficulties
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Address challenges requiring long-term commitment and strategic thinking
      • Maintain momentum despite significant obstacles
      • Offer sufficient context so that a 3rd party could understand the depth of the challenge
      • Demonstrate organizational influence and resilience
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Describe a challenge you faced in managing your team (e.g., performance issues, resource constraints)
      • Show how you supported your team members through difficult situations
  • Adaptability
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Clearly describe what changed or what disruption occurred
      • Explain why adaptation was necessary
      • Demonstrate quick assessment and decision-making
      • Show how you adjusted your plans or approach
      • Demonstrate flexibility and open-mindedness
      • Explain how your adaptability affected the outcome
      • Reflect on lessons learned about handling change?
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Lead the team's response to major, unexpected changes
      • Demonstrate rapid decision-making and effective communication
      • Show how you influenced team adaptation
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Lead adaptation across multiple teams
      • Anticipate and mitigate potential resistance
      • Offer a clear framework for making a decision and moving forward
      • Demonstrate strategic thinking in managing organizational change
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Adjust your management style to meet different needs
      • Effectively manage change within your team
      • Support team members through transitions
      • Align with the organization's goals, even if they weren't your top priority
  • Growth Mindset
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Describe a challenge, failure, setback, or critical feedback received
      • Demonstrate willingness to learn from mistakes
      • Show how you actively sought and used feedback
      • Explain specific steps taken to learn new skills
      • Highlight self-reflection and honest assessment
      • Describe how you applied new learning
      • Emphasize commitment to continuous learning
      • Show a positive attitude towards challenges
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Describe a significant failure/setback as a catalyst for growth
      • Demonstrate how you applied learning to achieve later success
      • Show leadership in promoting learning culture
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Identify and address critical organizational skill gaps
      • Champion a culture of learning and improvement
      • Demonstrate strategic thinking about development
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Foster a growth mindset within your team
      • Demonstrate continuous learning as a manager
      • Effectively develop and mentor team members
  • Collaboration and Teamwork
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Describe a situation where you worked effectively as part of a team
      • Highlight your contributions while acknowledging others
      • Explain how you communicated and collaborated
      • Show how you handled disagreements constructively
      • Demonstrate ability to share knowledge and support colleagues
      • Explain how you adapted to different work styles
      • Describe the team's overall success?
      • Reflect on what you learned about teamwork
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Foster cross-functional collaboration for strategic goals
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Break down silos across organizational boundaries
      • Demonstrate ability to influence across functions
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Facilitate collaboration within and between teams
      • Create structures for effective teamwork
      • Show evidence of uncovering the root cause of collaboration issues
  • Leadership
    • All levels (in-scope)
      • Describe a situation where you took initiative and guided others
      • Explain how you influenced and motivated others
      • Highlight your decision-making process
      • Take responsibility for outcomes
      • Demonstrate ability to inspire and support team members
      • Explain how you built consensus
      • Show effective delegation and feedback
      • Reflect on your leadership style and learnings
    • Senior (in-scope)
      • Describe a situation with significant organizational impact
      • Demonstrate influence without direct authority
      • Show how you built and maintained high-performing teams
    • Staff (out-of-scope)
      • Influence the strategic direction of major initiatives
      • Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders
      • Demonstrate strategic thinking and negotiation skills
    • Manager (out-of-scope)
      • Focus on coaching, mentoring, and developing team members
      • Create a positive and productive team environment
      • Demonstrate effective people management skills
  • Reference
    • The Software Engineer's Guidebook
